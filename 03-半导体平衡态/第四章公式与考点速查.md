# 第四章公式与考点速查

标签：#公式 #考点 #半导体平衡态 #Chapter4

## Carrier concentration

$$
n(E)=g_c(E)f_F(E)
$$

$$
p(E)=g_v(E)[1-f_F(E)]
$$

$$
n_0=N_c\exp\left[-\frac{E_c-E_F}{kT}\right]
$$

$$
p_0=N_v\exp\left[-\frac{E_F-E_v}{kT}\right]
$$

## Intrinsic semiconductor

$$
n_i^2=N_cN_v\exp\left[-\frac{E_g}{kT}\right]
$$

$$
E_{Fi}=\frac{E_c+E_v}{2}+\frac{1}{2}kT\ln\left(\frac{N_v}{N_c}\right)
$$

## Relative to intrinsic Fermi level

$$
n_0=n_i\exp\left[\frac{E_F-E_{Fi}}{kT}\right]
$$

$$
p_0=n_i\exp\left[-\frac{E_F-E_{Fi}}{kT}\right]
$$

## Mass action law

$$
n_0p_0=n_i^2
$$

## Charge neutrality

$$
n_0+N_A^-=p_0+N_D^+
$$

complete ionization：

$$
n_0+N_A=p_0+N_D
$$

## Majority carrier approximations

n-type：$n_0\approx N_D-N_A$，$p_0=n_i^2/n_0$。

p-type：$p_0\approx N_A-N_D$，$n_0=n_i^2/p_0$。

## Fermi level position

n-type：

$$
E_c-E_F=kT\ln\left(\frac{N_c}{n_0}\right)
$$

p-type：

$$
E_F-E_v=kT\ln\left(\frac{N_v}{p_0}\right)
$$
